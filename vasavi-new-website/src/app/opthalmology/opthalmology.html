Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I schedule an appointment for Ophthalmology services at Vasavi Hospitals?

    You can easily schedule an appointment by contacting our dedicated appointment line or using our online appointment booking system on our website.

  • What should I expect during my initial eye examination at Vasavi Hospitals?

    During your first visit, the ophthalmologist will conduct a comprehensive eye examination, discuss your eye health, and recommend appropriate treatments or interventions.

    Yes, our hospital is equipped with advanced technology and experienced ophthalmologists to perform a range of eye surgeries, including cataract surgery and cornea transplantation.

  • Do I need a referral to consult with an ophthalmologist at Vasavi Hospitals?

    No, you can directly schedule an appointment with our ophthalmology specialists without needing a referral for your convenience.
