Robotic Surgery in Gynecology with Da Vinci Xi

At Vasavi Hospitals, we offer cutting-edge gynecological care using the advanced Da Vinci Xi robotic surgical system. This technology enables our specialists to perform complex gynecological procedures with enhanced precision, minimal pain, and faster recovery times. Our goal is to provide safe, effective, and patient-friendly treatment options for women of all ages.

Why Choose Robotic Gynecology Surgery?

Unmatched Surgical Precision

The Da Vinci system provides magnified 3D vision and superior instrument control, allowing surgeons to perform delicate gynecological surgeries with extreme accuracy.

Minimally Invasive Procedures

Most procedures, including hysterectomy, myomectomy, and endometriosis surgery, are done through small incisions—resulting in less pain, minimal blood loss, and faster recovery.

Quick Return to Daily Life

Patients experience shorter hospital stays, less downtime, and a quicker return to normal routines compared to open surgery.

Improved Patient Outcomes

Robotic-assisted gynecological surgery reduces complications, improves recovery, and enhances reproductive organ preservation where possible.
