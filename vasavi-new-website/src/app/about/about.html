About Vasavi Hospitals

Welcome to Vasavi Hospitals, a premier healthcare institution committed to providing exceptional medical care with compassion and expertise. We pride ourselves on being your trusted healthcare partner, dedicated to your well-being and the well-being of your loved ones.

Vision

To be a global healthcare provider with compassion.

Mission

We provide world-class healthcare to the community at large by harnessing modern technology, medical expertise and state-of-the-art infrastructure. We aim to achieve clinical excellence with foremost importance to patient care without compromising on quality at an affordable price.

Values

  • Excellence -In Clinical & Non-Clinical services
  • Enthusiasm - Be inspired to save & enrich life
  • Ethics - Follow the code of medical ethics
  • Empathy - To understand & feel

Our CEO's Message

It is an honour beyond words, for me to lead this great organization and a privilege to be a part of an exceptional team of people who are passionate about providing the best care for patients. As a Trust hospital, we feel a deep sense of pride and commitment to all those who place their trust in our care. Our primary focus is to deliver exceptional care that exceeds national standards in quality, safety and patient satisfaction.

Even though the bricks and mortar, the technical equipment and the system resources are the backbone of our organization, the heart and soul of this organization is the “PEOPLE” who give so much of their time and their talent to help those in need overcome their individual health obstacles. It is these physicians, nurses and other care givers who each and every day blend the miracle of compassion with the marvel of technology to make a real difference in the lives of our patients.

We are committed to earn the trust and faith you place in us and are honoured to be able to participate in your healthcare needs. While we take pride in what we have accomplished, we remain resolute in our focus to address the future healthcare needs of our communities.

So, when you or your loved ones need a hospital, you can count on us! We’re here when you need us.

Srivatsan Chakravarthy,

CEO

Our Core Values

Compassion

We approach patient care with empathy and understanding, ensuring that every individual feels valued and supported on their healthcare journey.

Excellence

Our commitment to excellence drives us to deliver the highest standards of medical care, exceeding the expectations of our patients.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards, ensuring transparency and trust in all our interactions with patients and their families.

Innovation

We continuously seek innovative solutions, medical technologies, and treatments to provide the best care possible.

Teamwork

Collaboration and teamwork are the cornerstones of our approach. We believe that by working together, we can achieve the best outcomes for our patients.

Our Promise

We promise to provide you with the best possible care, treating every patient with respect, empathy, and dignity. Your health and comfort are our top priorities, and we are here to support you through every step of your healthcare journey.

Vasavi Hospitals is more than just a healthcare institution; we are your partner in health and well-being. We look forward to serving you and your family, and to being a part of your path to a healthier and happier life. Thank you for choosing Vasavi Hospitals as your trusted healthcare provider.
