What is Total Hip Replacement?

Total Hip Replacement (THR) is a procedure where the damaged or worn-out parts of your hip joint are replaced with artificial components called prostheses. It helps restore pain-free motion and stability in people with arthritis, fractures, or chronic hip pain.

If hip pain limits your daily movement or disturbs your sleep, it may be time to consult our experts. At Vasavi Hospitals, we offer both Conventional Hip Replacement and Mako Robotic-Assisted Surgery — for smoother movement, accurate alignment, and lasting comfort.

Recognize the Symptoms. Restore Your Mobility.

Don’t ignore persistent hip pain or stiffness. Early consultation can prevent further joint damage and help you regain mobility faster with advanced surgical care.

Common Symptoms

  • Hip pain that worsens with walking or movement
  • Difficulty sitting, standing, or bending comfortably
  • Grinding or stiffness in the hip joint

Your Hip Replacement Journey

Comprehensive Care from Consultation to Recovery

1

Consultation & Pre-Surgery Evaluation

Our orthopedic specialists review your X-rays and assess hip mobility. For robotic surgery, a 3D scan maps your hip for personalized implant planning.

Under anesthesia, the surgeon performs conventional or robotic-assisted hip replacement to restore smooth movement and joint stability.

With physiotherapy and gradual exercises, patients regain flexibility and confidence. Robotic patients often recover faster with improved balance and posture.
