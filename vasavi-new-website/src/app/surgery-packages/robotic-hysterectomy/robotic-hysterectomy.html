What is a Hysterectomy?

A Hysterectomy is a surgical procedure to remove the uterus (womb). It’s often recommended for women with fibroids, severe endometriosis, adenomyosis, uterine prolapse, or abnormal bleeding. At Vasavi Hospitals, Bangalore, we offer Laparoscopic and Robotic Hysterectomy (Da Vinci Xi) to ensure safe, precise, and faster recovery.

Why It’s Done

Treats fibroids, heavy bleeding, pelvic pain, and endometriosis. Minimally invasive techniques ensure quicker recovery, less pain, and better outcomes.

  • Relief from chronic pelvic pain
  • Improved quality of life
  • Minimal scarring and blood loss

Laparoscopic Hysterectomy

Uses tiny incisions and a camera-guided approach for precise removal of the uterus. Offers minimal scarring, short hospital stay, and fast recovery.

  • Quick healing
  • Day-care/short stay
  • Faster return to routine

Robotic Hysterectomy (Da Vinci Xi)

Provides 3D HD vision, enhanced precision, and better control during surgery. Ideal for complex cases where accuracy and safety are critical.

  • High-definition 3D visualization
  • Superior dexterity & precision
  • Minimal pain and blood loss

Robotic vs. Laparoscopic Hysterectomy Surgery: What’s the Difference?

Both are minimally invasive procedures for uterus removal, but the Da Vinci Xi Robotic System offers greater precision, control, and faster recovery.

Robotic Hysterectomy (Da Vinci Xi)

  • +

    3D HD Vision: Provides better visibility for precise dissection and suturing.

  • +

    Superior Dexterity: Robotic arms mimic human wrist motion for enhanced accuracy.

  • +

    Less Pain & Blood Loss: Smaller incisions ensure minimal tissue trauma.

Laparoscopic Hysterectomy

  • +

    2D Vision: Reliable but offers limited depth perception compared to robotic systems.

  • +

    Excellent Results: Proven effective with quick healing and minimal scars.

  • +

    Safe & Cost-Effective: Ideal for most cases of uterine conditions.
