What is a Cholecystectomy?

A Cholecystectomy is a gallbladder removal surgery performed to treat gallstones and gallbladder inflammation. The gallbladder stores bile that helps in digestion. When stones block the bile duct, it causes pain, nausea, and indigestion. Vasavi Hospitals offers both Laparoscopic and Robotic Cholecystectomy in Bangalore for safe, precise, and fast recovery.

Why It’s Done

Treats gallstones, acute/chronic cholecystitis, and biliary colic. Minimally invasive laparoscopic gallbladder removal ensures shorter hospital stays and quick return to daily life.

  • Less pain & minimal scarring
  • Faster recovery (3–5 days)
  • Lower risk of complications

Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy

Also known as keyhole gallbladder surgery, it uses tiny incisions and a high-definition camera. This Laparoscopic Gallbladder Surgery in Bangalore is safe, effective, and widely trusted.

  • Tiny incisions
  • Day-care/short stay
  • Quick return to normal life

Robotic Cholecystectomy (Da Vinci Xi)

Robotic Gallbladder Surgery offers 3D HD vision, superior precision, and enhanced control. It’s ideal for complex cases requiring extreme accuracy and minimal tissue trauma.

  • Enhanced dexterity & control
  • Stable and tremor-free movements
  • Best outcomes for complex cases

Robotic vs. Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy Surgery: What's the Difference?

Both are minimally invasive surgeries for gallbladder removal, but the Da Vinci Xi Robotic Surgery System offers superior precision, 3D vision, and faster recovery compared to traditional laparoscopy.

  • +
