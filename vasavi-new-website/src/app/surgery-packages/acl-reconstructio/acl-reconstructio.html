What is ACL Reconstruction?

ACL Reconstruction is a surgical procedure to repair or replace a torn Anterior Cruciate Ligament (ACL) - one of the main ligaments that stabilize the knee. It is commonly required after sports injuries, accidents or sudden twisting movements that cause knee instability or pain.

At Vasavi Hospitals, our expert orthopedic surgeons perform Arthroscopic ACL Reconstruction, a minimally invasive procedure that uses small incisions for faster healing, minimal pain and better mobility.
