When Appendix Pain Becomes an Emergency

Appendicitis pain usually begins near the navel and shifts to the lower right abdomen — often getting worse with movement, coughing, or touch.

If the pain is severe or recurring, it’s important not to delay treatment. Robotic Appendectomy is a safe, precise, and minimally invasive way to remove the infected appendix before complications arise.
