What is CT Angiography?

CT Angiography (CTA) is an advanced, non-invasive imaging test that uses CT scanning and contrast dye to visualize blood vessels and detect blockages, clots or narrowing in arteries and veins. It helps diagnose conditions affecting the heart, brain, kidneys, lungs and limbs with high precision.

At Vasavi Hospitals, our radiology department uses state-of-the-art multi-slice CT scanners that provide detailed 3D images of blood vessels, allowing accurate diagnosis and early detection of cardiovascular diseases.

If you experience chest pain, leg pain or symptoms of poor blood circulation, talk to our specialists to determine if CT Angiography is right for you.

Know the Signs. Choose the Right Test.

Vascular diseases often progress silently until they become serious. At Vasavi Hospitals, we help identify underlying issues early through accurate CT Angiography scans, enabling timely and effective treatment.

When is CT Angiography Recommended?

  • Chest pain or suspected coronary artery disease
  • Stroke or transient ischemic attack (TIA) symptoms
  • Pain or numbness in legs due to peripheral artery disease
  • Aneurysm detection in brain or abdomen
  • Evaluation before or after vascular surgery

Test Options

At Vasavi Hospitals, CT Angiography can be performed for:

  • Coronary CT Angiography - Evaluates heart and coronary arteries
  • Cerebral CT Angiography - Checks blood flow in brain vessels
  • Peripheral CT Angiography - Examines arteries in arms or legs
  • Pulmonary CT Angiography - Detects blockages in lungs

Each test is quick, safe and provides high-resolution images for precise diagnosis.
