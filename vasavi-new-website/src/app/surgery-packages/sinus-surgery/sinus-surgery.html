What is Sinus Surgery?

Sinus surgery is a procedure to clear blocked sinus passages, improving airflow and relieving long-term sinus infections, headaches, or breathing difficulties. It is often recommended when medications or nasal sprays no longer provide lasting relief.

At Vasavi Hospitals, our ENT specialists perform Endoscopic Sinus Surgery using advanced Debrider technique - a modern, minimally invasive method that removes diseased tissue and restores natural sinus drainage with precision and minimal discomfort.

If you suffer from frequent sinus infections, facial pressure, or nasal blockage, meet our ENT experts for accurate diagnosis and effective treatment.

Know the Signs. Choose the Right Care.

Many people mistake sinus pain or congestion for a common cold. At Vasavi Hospitals, we help identify chronic sinus conditions early and provide advanced endoscopic treatment for long-term relief.

Common Symptoms

  • Persistent nasal congestion or stuffiness
  • Repeated sinus infections or facial pain
  • Headache and pressure around the eyes or forehead
  • Loss of smell or difficulty breathing through the nose
