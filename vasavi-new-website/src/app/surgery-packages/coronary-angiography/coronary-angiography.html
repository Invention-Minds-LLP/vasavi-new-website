What is Coronary Angiography?

Coronary Angiography is a diagnostic procedure that helps visualize the arteries supplying blood to your heart. It is performed to detect blockages, narrowing or other abnormalities that may lead to chest pain or heart attacks.

At Vasavi Hospitals, our experienced cardiologists use advanced catheter-based technology and digital imaging systems to ensure accurate and safe results. This test plays a key role in diagnosing coronary artery disease and planning the right treatment, such as angioplasty or bypass surgery.

If you experience chest discomfort, breathlessness or a history of heart disease, speak to our specialists to know if a Coronary Angiography is right for you.

Know the Signs. Choose the Right Test.

Heart disease often develops silently, showing symptoms only when the condition becomes serious. At Vasavi Hospitals, we perform Coronary Angiography to detect heart blockages early and help prevent complications.

When is Coronary Angiography Recommended?

  • Chest pain (angina) or shortness of breath
  • Abnormal ECG or stress test results
  • History of heart attack or coronary disease
  • Unexplained chest discomfort or dizziness
  • Evaluation before cardiac surgery or angioplasty
