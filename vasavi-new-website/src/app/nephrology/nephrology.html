Robotic Surgery in Nephrology with Da Vinci Xi

At Vasavi Hospitals, Banashankari, Bangalore, our nephrology department uses the advanced Da Vinci Xi robotic surgical system to treat complex kidney and urinary tract conditions. This technology allows our nephrology doctors to perform delicate procedures with enhanced accuracy, minimal pain and quicker recovery - helping patients return to healthy living faster.

What Makes Our Robotic Nephrology Surgery Special?

Unmatched Surgical Precision

The Da Vinci system provides magnified 3D vision and superior control, enabling surgeons to operate on kidney and urinary tract conditions with extreme accuracy.

Minimally Invasive Procedures

Kidney surgeries, including tumor removal, ureteric reconstruction and partial nephrectomy, are performed through small incisions - ensuring less pain, reduced blood loss and faster recovery.

Quick Return to Daily Life

Patients experience shorter hospital stays and a quicker return to normal routines compared to open kidney surgery.

Improved Patient Outcomes

Robotic-assisted nephrology surgery lowers complications, improves recovery and preserves kidney function whenever possible.
