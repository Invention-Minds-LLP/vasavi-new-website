ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat) Care at Vasavi Hospitals, Banashankari

Your health deserves the best care, and at Vasavi Hospitals, Banashankari, we make it our mission to help you feel better, faster. Our expert ENT specialists treat everything - from a blocked nose, severe sore throat, ear pain, tinnitus or earwax build-up to complex sinus and voice problems - with skill and compassion.

With cutting-edge technology and a patient-first approach, we ensure you and your loved ones can breathe easier, hear clearly and live without discomfort.

Our ENT Treatments

We provide complete care for your ear, nose and throat concerns:

Ear Examination and Treatment

Safe removal of earwax, ear cleaning, treatment for ear infections and clogged ears.

Nasal and Sinus Surgeries

Solutions for blocked nose, nasal polyps, deviated septum and chronic sinusitis.

Throat and Voice Disorder Management

Treatment for sore throat, laryngitis, voice changes and swallowing problems.

Head and Neck Surgery

Advanced care for head & neck conditions, including tumors and thyroid issues.

Sleep Apnea and Snoring Solutions

Effective treatments to improve sleep quality and reduce snoring.

Allergy and Immunotherapy

Relief for nasal allergies, rhinitis and breathing issues with latest immunotherapy methods.

Skin Prick Test & SLIT Test

Quick allergy testing to find triggers and provide personalized allergy solutions.
