FAQs

1.How do I book an appointment with a cardiologist at Vasavi Hospitals?
You can book an appointment easily by visiting our Book an Appointment page or calling our 24x7 helpline at 080 71 500 500. Our team will guide you to the right cardiology specialist.
2.What conditions are treated by the cardiology department?
Our cardiology department treats all major heart-related conditions including heart attacks, blocked arteries, high blood pressure, irregular heartbeats, heart failure, and congenital heart problems.
3.What should I expect during my first consultation?
During your first visit, the cardiologist will discuss your symptoms, check your medical history, perform a physical examination, and may recommend tests like ECG or echocardiography. Based on the results, you will receive a clear and personalized treatment plan.
4.Does Vasavi Hospitals offer advanced heart procedures?
Yes, we provide advanced treatments including angioplasty, bypass surgery, valve repair or replacement, and device implantations like pacemakers – all using the latest technology for safe and effective outcomes.
5.Are there non-surgical treatment options available?
Absolutely. Many heart conditions can be managed with medications, lifestyle changes, regular monitoring, and cardiac rehabilitation programs designed to improve long-term heart health.
6.What follow-up care is provided after treatment?
We offer personalized follow-up care which includes regular check-ups, guidance on medicines, diet and exercise advice, and support to help you return to normal life quickly and reduce the risk of future heart problems.
