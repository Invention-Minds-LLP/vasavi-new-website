FAQs

1. How do I book an appointment with a urologist at Vasavi Hospitals?
You can easily schedule an appointment by visiting our Book an Appointment page or calling our 24x7 helpline at 080 71 500 500. Our support team will connect you with the right urology specialist.
2. What conditions are treated by the urology department at Vasavi Hospitals?
Our urology team provides expert care for kidney stones, prostate disorders, urinary tract infections (UTIs), bladder problems, male infertility, erectile dysfunction, cancers of the urinary system, and congenital urological conditions.
3. What should I expect during my first consultation with a urologist at Vasavi Hospitals?
During your first consultation, the urologist will take a detailed medical history, perform a physical examination, and may recommend diagnostic tests such as ultrasound, urine tests, or cystoscopy. Based on the findings, you will receive a personalized treatment plan.
4. Does Vasavi Hospitals provide robotic-assisted urology surgeries?
Yes. Vasavi Hospitals is equipped with the Da Vinci Xi robotic system, one of the most advanced technologies for urological procedures. Robotic surgery ensures greater precision, smaller incisions, less pain, minimal blood loss, and faster recovery for patients undergoing complex urological treatments.
5. What surgical and non-surgical treatments are available in urology at Vasavi Hospitals?
Our hospital offers a full range of treatments including :
  • Minimally invasive procedures for kidney stones (LASER, PCNL, URS, RIRS)
  • Prostate surgeries (TURP, HoLEP, Robotic Prostatectomy)
  • Bladder and urethral surgeries
  • Urological cancer surgeries
  • Male infertility and andrology services
6. What rehabilitation or follow-up care is provided after urology surgery?
Vasavi Hospitals provides personalized post-surgery care, including pain management, physiotherapy if required, lifestyle guidance, and long-term follow-ups to ensure complete recovery and prevention of recurrence.
