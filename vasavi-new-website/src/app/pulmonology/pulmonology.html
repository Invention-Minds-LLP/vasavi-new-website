FAQs

1. Do you treat children’s lung and breathing problems?
Yes. Our pulmonologists are trained in pediatric care, managing conditions like asthma, chest infections and sleep-related breathing disorders.
2. How can I book an appointment with a pulmonologist at Vasavi Hospitals?
Call us directly at 080 71 500 500 or fill out our online appointment form to schedule your consultation today.
3. Who should consult a pulmonologist?
If you have frequent coughing, breathing difficulty, chest tightness or chronic lung conditions, it’s time to consult a pulmonologist.
4. How long does recovery take after lung treatment or surgery?
Recovery time depends on the type of treatment. With minimally invasive and robotic options, most patients recover faster.
5. Are smoking-related lung problems treated at Vasavi Hospitals?
Yes. We provide complete care for smoker’s lungs, COPD, emphysema and chronic bronchitis, with ongoing support to improve health.
6. Do you provide advanced lung testing at Vasavi Hospitals?
Yes, at Vasavi Hospitals we offer pulmonary function tests, chest imaging, bronchoscopy and lung cancer screening for accurate diagnosis.
7. Do you provide sleep apnea testing and care?
Yes, at Vasavi Hospitals we conduct sleep studies and provide treatment plans to help you sleep and breathe better.
8. Do you have senior pulmonologists for complex cases?
Yes. Our team includes experienced pulmonology doctors who manage advanced cases like interstitial lung disease and pulmonary hypertension.
